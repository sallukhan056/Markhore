It all comes down to the craft

Markhor shoes are the product of centuries old craftsmanship, simple design and communal thinking. The quality of our shoes derives from the interaction between our craftsmen and designers. Markhor is built on the idea of connecting the craftsmen directly with the people who value their art.
Indigenous craftsmanshi

In the old city of Lahore and Okara, we find and work with some of the most skilled craftsmen on earth. For 1800 years, these artisans have perfected the art of leather craftsmanship, handing skills down in each generation.
